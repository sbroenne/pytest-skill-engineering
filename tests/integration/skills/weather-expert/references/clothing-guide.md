# Weather Clothing Guide

## Temperature-Based Clothing

### Cold (< 40°F / 4°C)
- Heavy winter coat or down jacket
- Gloves and hat/beanie
- Scarf or neck warmer
- Thermal underlayers
- Warm boots

### Cool (40-55°F / 4-13°C)
- Medium jacket or fleece
- Long pants
- Light sweater or hoodie
- Closed-toe shoes

### Mild (55-70°F / 13-21°C)
- Light jacket (for mornings/evenings)
- Long pants or jeans
- T-shirt or light long-sleeve
- Optional: light cardigan

### Warm (70-85°F / 21-29°C)
- T-shirt or light blouse
- Shorts or lightweight pants
- Sandals or breathable shoes
- Sunglasses

### Hot (> 85°F / 29°C)
- Loose, light-colored clothing
- Shorts and tank top
- Hat for sun protection
- Plenty of water

## UV Index Guidelines

| UV Index | Risk Level | Recommendations |
|----------|------------|-----------------|
| 0-2 | Low | Minimal protection needed |
| 3-5 | Moderate | Wear sunglasses, use SPF 30 |
| 6-7 | High | SPF 30+, seek shade during midday |
| 8-10 | Very High | SPF 50+, avoid midday sun, wear a hat |
| 11+ | Extreme | Take all precautions, limit outdoor exposure |

## Precipitation Preparation

- **20-40% chance**: Bring compact umbrella just in case
- **40-60% chance**: Waterproof jacket recommended
- **60-80% chance**: Full rain gear, waterproof shoes
- **80%+ chance**: Plan indoor alternatives, waterproof everything

## Wind Considerations

- **10-20 mph**: Light jacket for wind chill
- **20-30 mph**: Windbreaker essential, secure loose items
- **30+ mph**: Consider rescheduling outdoor activities
